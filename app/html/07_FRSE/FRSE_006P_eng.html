Account Deletion

Before deleting your Child Safety Guardian account, please review the following information:

  • Once deleted, all personal information and usage records will be permanently removed and cannot be restored.
  • All service data, including your child's location information and alert history, will be deleted.
  • Premium subscriptions are not automatically canceled when the account is deleted.
  • You must cancel your subscription directly via the [App Store] or [Google Play] subscription management.
  • If you delete your account without canceling your store subscription, charges may continue.
  • According to relevant laws, certain payment/transaction records may be retained for a set period.
