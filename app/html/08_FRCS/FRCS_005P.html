1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information We collect and process: • Parent/guardian account details (email, phone number, password) • Child profile information (name/nickname, birth year, device identifiers) 1.2 Location Information To provide core safety features, we collect: • GPS coordinates • Wi-Fi signal information • Cell-tower identifiers • Motion/acceleration data • Background location data (even when the app is closed, if permission is granted) 1.3 Device & Usage Information We collect device and diagnostic data, including: • Device model, OS version, unique identifiers • Battery level, network status • App activity logs and crash logs Retention: Logs are kept for 90 days and deleted or anonymized thereafter. 1.4 Surrounding Sound (Ambient Audio) If the parent activates the “Live Surrounding Sound” feature: • Short-duration audio samples are temporarily captured by the child’s device and transmitted in real time to the parent’s device • When this feature is activated, the child’s device will display a clear on-screen notification indicating that ambient audio is being shared, so the child is aware that the microphone is in use for real-time audio transmission. By enabling this feature, the parent confirms compliance with all applicable audio-recording, consent, and surveillance laws, including two-party/all-party consent jurisdictions, where applicable. We do not store, retain, or archive any audio data on our servers after transmission is completed. However, audio received on the parent’s device may be stored on that device depending on the parent’s settings and installed applications, which is outside of our control. Any storage, copying, or sharing of audio on the parent’s device is solely under the parent’s control and responsibility. ATON has no access to, and cannot delete or manage, any such stored audio. The parent or guardian is solely responsible for using this feature in a way that complies with the monitoring, recording, privacy, and other applicable laws in the country or region where they and the child are located. We do not provide legal advice and we are not responsible for any unlawful use of this feature. 1.5 Communication & Alerts We collect: • SOS events • Geofence events • Parent-child notification logs • Movement and activity history (retained for 90 days) 1.6 Payment Information Payments are handled through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store full payment or credit card information.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary: • Location history: retained for 90 days, then deleted or anonymized • Audio samples: never stored; deleted immediately after transmission • App activity & error logs: retained for 90 days • Account data: fully deleted 30 days after account deletion request (grace period allows accidental recovery and prevents fraud) 6.1 Deletion Process We follow an automated data deletion process. Personal data stored on our servers is automatically deleted or anonymized once the applicable retention period expires, without any manual intervention. • Location history, movement data, app activity logs, and diagnostic data are automatically deleted or anonymized within 90 days of collection. • When a user requests account deletion (by selecting the “Delete Account” option in the in-app Settings menu or by contacting us at privacy@atoncorp.com), all associated account information is placed into a 30-day grace period to prevent accidental loss or fraud, after which it is permanently erased from our production systems. • Certain copies of data may remain in encrypted backup archives for up to 90 days after deletion, for system integrity and disaster-recovery purposes; such copies are automatically purged as part of our scheduled backup overwrite process. All deletion is performed automatically by the Service infrastructure, and no further retention or manual review occurs once deletion is triggered. We do not retain deleted personal data beyond the specified retention duration, except where longer retention is legally required (e.g., fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or compliance). Users may request full deletion at any time. Certain data may remain in backups for up to 90 days for security and disaster-recovery purposes, after which it is automatically purged. Furthermore, certain data may be retained for a longer period where required by applicable laws, including, but not limited to, for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, and/or compliance purposes.
